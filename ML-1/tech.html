The Technology We Use

Discover the cutting-edge technologies that power our advanced brain analysis services.

MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging)

  • Non-invasive imaging technique
  • High-resolution brain scans
  • Detailed anatomical structure
  • Detection of abnormalities
  • Real-time brain activity monitoring

EEG (Electroencephalography)

  • Records electrical activity of the brain
  • Helps diagnose neurological disorders
  • Monitors brain activity during sleep
  • Assesses cognitive functions
  • Used in epilepsy treatment

CT (Computed Tomography) Scan

  • Combines X-ray images for detailed brain structure
  • Quick and accurate imaging
  • Detects bleeding, tumors, and injuries
  • Guides surgical planning
  • Monitors treatment effectiveness
